About

Independent prescribing services based in York, supporting aesthetic practice. I work in collaboration with aesthetic practitioners to provide prescribing following appropriate consultation and holistic clinical assessment.

Patient safety and individual need are central to all prescribing decisions. Assessments consider the wider health, wellbeing, and circumstances of the individual, ensuring prescribing is appropriate and within scope of professional practice. All prescribing is undertaken in line with legal, ethical, and professional standards.

How the Service Works

Prescribing support is provided in collaboration with aesthetic practitioners. A consultation and holistic clinical assessment are undertaken to ensure prescribing is appropriate for the individual.

Clinical judgement and patient safety guide all prescribing decisions, which are informed by holistic assessment and individual need.

Who This Service Is For

This service is intended for registered aesthetic practitioners who require independent prescribing support as part of their practice. Prescribing is not offered directly to members of the public.

Professional Credentials

I am a registered healthcare professional and qualified Independent Prescriber, registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC). Prescribing practice is maintained through ongoing professional development and reflective practice, in line with professional standards.

Availability & Appointments

I provide a mobile prescribing service and can attend agreed locations as required. Appointments are currently available on Thursdays in the Acomb area.
